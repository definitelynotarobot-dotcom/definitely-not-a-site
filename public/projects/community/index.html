In the Community

At Definitely Not a Robot, our approach starts with identifying projects that local nonprofits or schools lack the resources to pursue. There’s no shortage of ways to help. We’ll find a project that fits your team best. Examples include:

Building benches, picnic tables, garden beds, and more.

Mulching, pruning, planting, and invasive species removal.

Painting classrooms, hallways, murals, and community spaces.

Maybe you know a park or school that needs something like the above? We’re happy to work with you to facilitate projects right in your neighborhood.
