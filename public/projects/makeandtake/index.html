Make and Take

Participants will be guided through construction of a project coupled with some light educational material on maintenance and proper usage.

Definitely Not a Robot projects are known for being high quality, well built, and useful.

Our workshops create a variety of items which are typically donated to local schools or nonprofits.

Some of these creations actually serve the community best when they are taken home by their creator. You!

At the end of the project, participants can take their creations home to extend their impact or pass them along to one of our nonprofit partners who will put them to good use.
