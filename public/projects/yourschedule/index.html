On Your Schedule

A few of our projects have been recently redesigned to allow for teams or individuals to complete them on their own schedule.

We provide accurate estimates for completion time, and participants can set their own schedule that works for them.

Once completed, simply let us know and we’ll coordinate pick-up and ensure delivery to the recipient organization.
