Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making our website content and experience accessible and user-friendly for everyone. We strive to provide a positive experience for all visitors, regardless of ability or the adaptive technology being used.

Conformance Status

The Definitely Not a Robot website strives to conform with Level AA of the World Wide Web Consortium’s (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1.

WCAG guidelines define three levels of conformance: A, AA, and AAA. By meeting Level AA, we aim to ensure the site is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust across various devices and assistive technologies.

Technical Information

The accessibility of this website relies on the following technologies to work with the combination of web browsers and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript

We rely on the standard accessibility features of these technologies and the semantic structure provided by the Hugo framework to meet conformance standards.

Known Limitations

We aim for fully accessible content; however, despite our best efforts, we recognize that there may be some known limitations. If you encounter an issue, please contact us immediately so we can address it.

  • Third-Party Content: Some embedded third-party content (e.g., social media feeds or external booking widgets, if added in the future) may not fully conform to WCAG standards. We advocate for accessible tools whenever possible.
