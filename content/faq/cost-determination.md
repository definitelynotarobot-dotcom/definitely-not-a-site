Our pricing is all-inclusive and transparent, based on the complexity and scale of the project. 

#### Cost Factors

Pricing is primarily determined by:
- Group size
- Project type 
- Event location 
- Facilitation Time
- Customization

#### Transparency

- **Quote Provided:** We provide a comprehensive, fixed quote upfront that covers all aspects of the event, ensuring no unexpected costs.
- **Budget Alignment:** We are happy to develop projects around a specific budget to maximize the measurable impact your team can achieve.
